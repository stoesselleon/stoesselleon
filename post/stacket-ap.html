Thought
I just discovered Stacket. A neat service wich provides functionality that a lot of people learning about webflow are searching for. Hosting somewhere you want, not on Webflow, while still having the Webflow Designer with all it features INCLUDING webflow cms. Is it a dream? Yes and No. I love how it works, I even use it for my own blog as you’ve probably discovered because of the tag down right. But I would still never use it for a client in our agency. Setting it up are so too many extra points you have to take care. Even though it’s quite simple Servers, Hosting, File-Structure and so one have to be taken care of. For specific usecases it’s very good. Maybe if you want to host GDPR compliant, that’s nice. If you have a selfmade backend or app you want to connect it to, also great. But the “normal” Website works fine with just the Webflow hosting. Also pricing is a point here for me, but that’s something you can decide on your own. Be sure to check it out and have the possibilities with stacket on your radar! LINK TO Stacket What are your thoughts? I’d love to connect on Twitter!
